Class 10.5

The transition from class 10 to Class 11 is not easy in many ways. The syllabus becomes more vast and abstract. Generally, after Class 10, the student have to make a very important decision of choosng a major from Engineering, Mediacal, Commerce and Arts. There's a perception that brighter students should choose either Engineering or Medical, but it should be entirely student's decision which he/she should take based on his/her interest.
Class 10.5 is basically an attempt to make these decisions better.
It will provide Mathematics of level between Class 10 and Class 11. This will take care of all your competitve needs like NTSE, JEE Mains and even JEE Advanced.
